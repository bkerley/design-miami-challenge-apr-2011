Design Miami Challenge

(codename: FUN DMC)

The Design Miami Challenge for April 2011 is to design and build a website for a fictional Canadian-cuisine restaurant here in Coconut Grove. We will provide you with the menu, hours, and address, and you get to design and build the site. At the May meetup, you'll present your idea, we'll all vote on a winner, and you'll win a prize of my choosing.

For mid-competition updates, follow FUN DMC on Twitter.

Restaurant Information

"The Grand Canadian Cafe" is a new bistro in Coconut Grove featuring a selection of Canadian/American fusion cuisine celebrating the culture, heritage, and spirit of America's neighbor to the North.

The restaurant is located at 2975 Oak Ave, Miami Florida, 33133 (this is actually Fire Station #8), is open for breakfast, lunch, and dinner; and has a bar open until midnight. While many people think of the big eastern metropolis of Toronto when they think "Canada," also think of Calgary, home of the largest rodeo in the world; Banff, a resort town in the Rocky Mountains; and Vancouver, Canada's gateway to Asia. You might also think about Saskatchewan, which grows much of Canada's food; and the Northwest Territories, home of the TV series "Ice Road Truckers."

Menu

Breakfast

  • The Vancouver: a whole-Saskatchwean-wheat bagel with Canadian cream cheese, fresh chives, and smoked Salmon from British Columbia.
  • The Calgary: a whole-Saskatchewan-wheat bagel with beef sausage, bacon, and eggs.
  • The Saskatoon: Belgian waffles with Saskatoon berry syrup.
  • The Lumberjack: Five pancakes with hearty Canadian maple syrup.

Sandwiches

All sandwiches are available on white, whole-wheat, or rye bread from Saskatchewan wheat.
